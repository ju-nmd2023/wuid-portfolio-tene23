Coffetable Book

This project was created to show of two spreads in coffetable book about a designer of choice, in this case Alessandro Michele. Color-theory, typography, layout and imagination created this project. The black backround was chosen to contribute to a bit of drama, and the red text compliments Michele's red t-shirt, as well as the red hat Michele can be seen wearing on the top image to the right, and the red letter T. The second spread to the right continues with a red and green theme, and black thin border to frame the content. Created in October 2023
