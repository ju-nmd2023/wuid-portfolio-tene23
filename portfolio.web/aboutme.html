ABOUT ME

My name is Nellie Thell and I grew up in a small city called Tranås, located on the right side of Vättern. My interest for graphic design and webdevelopment was not always clear. After I graduated from highschool I moved to the westcoast to begin studies at university, a program called Digital media, because I was vey interested in their filmmaking courses, although courses within graphic design and webdevelopment and design was also included. During the first year I realized this school was not for me but I really enjoyed graphic design and webdevelopment. When the summer came I dropped of, sent several application to other universities and got accepted to School of engineering in Jönköping, the New media design program.
